Namaste! Welcome to Gurkha-Oven in Spalding. Founded in with the ambition to open a chain of authentic Indian and Nepalese restaurants in the heart of United Kingdom, the first Gurkha-Oven is opened in Spalding. We serve both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. The Tandoori and Curry dishes are the specialty of the house and bring your taste buds in ecstasy. Do you like your foods spicy or mild? It is not so crazy to think that Gurkha-Oven can offer everyone their own choice. At Gurkha-Oven, we believe in quality and authenticity. We offer authentic delicious Indian and Nepalese cuisine. Our mission is to offer the best Indian and Nepalese cuisine in England. Visit Gurkha-Oven and make your dining experience memorable. We are also pleased to introduce you to a new taste which is reflective of unique Gurkha Nepalese & Indian cuisine. Born in the mountains of the Himalayas this has become a favourite style of food throughout the world. Gurkha-Oven Restaurant is located in the heart of United Kingdom. You can easily reach Gurkha-Oven Restaurant by car and public transport.
